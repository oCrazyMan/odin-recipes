Enchiladas Verdes

How to make Enchilda Verdes

The traditional green enchiladas with chicken, a typical Mexican dish, you can have for lunch or dinner, it is very rich and easy to make.

Ingredients

For the Salsa

  • 2 lbs tomatillos (16)
  • 4 Serrano Peppers
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 Onion Rough Chopped
  • 4 Cups water
  • 1 Cilantro
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Enchiladas

  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 2 cups shredded chicken
  • 12 corn tortillas

For the Toppings

  • Crema mexicana as desired
  • crumbled queso fresco (or cotija)
  • diced raw onion as desired
  • chopped cilantro as desired

Instructions

Salsa

  1. Remove the husks from the tomatillos and rinse.
  2. Place the tomatillos, garlic, serranos, and onion in a stockpot.
  3. If looking to make a milder sauce, remove the seeds from the chile peppers.
  4. Pour water into the stock pot.
  5. Enough to cover the tomatillos.
  6. Cover and bring to a boil.
  7. Simmer for 7-10 minutes.
  8. Discard Water
  9. Remove the ingredients from the stock pot and into the blender.
  10. Add salt and cilantro to the blender.
  11. Blend until smooth.
  12. Heat up oil in the same stock pot used to cook the salsa.
  13. Pour in the salsa verde and cook for 10 minutes.
  14. Stir occasionally.
  15. Set aside until ready to use.

Enchiladas

  1. Heat up oil in a frying pan.
  2. Fry the tortillas. 1 minute on each side.
  3. Place on a paper towel to drain any excess oil.
  4. Repeat with all the tortillas.
  5. To assemble, place a tortilla in a baking dish and using your best judgement add about 2 tbsps shredded chicken.
  6. Roll up the tortilla.
  7. Make sure the seam side is down.
  8. Repeat until with the remaining tortillas.
  9. Heat up oven to 375 degrees F.
  10. Cover the tortillas with aluminum foil and place in the oven.
  11. Bake for 15 minutes.
  12. Keep in a warm oven until ready to serve.
  13. Pour a generous amount of the salsa verde on the plate.
  14. Add the chicken enchiladas to a plate.
  15. Pour more sauce on top.
  16. Add the desired toppings - crema mexicana, queso fresco, cilantro, and onion.
  17. Serve and eat right away!
